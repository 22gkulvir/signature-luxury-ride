Who We Are

Dallas's Premier Black Car Service

Signature Luxury Ride Inc. was founded with a single mission: to bring truly first-class ground transportation to the Dallas–Fort Worth Metroplex. We saw a gap between basic rideshare services and the professional, polished experience that executives, families, and travelers deserve.

Based in Garland, TX, we serve all of DFW — from downtown Dallas to Plano, Frisco, Arlington, and everywhere in between. Our fleet of immaculately maintained black SUVs and our team of professional chauffeurs are dedicated to one thing: making your ride exceptional.

Whether it's a 5 AM airport run or a black-tie event across town, we show up — on time, prepared, and proud to represent the Signature standard.

Our Core Values

Professionalism

From the moment you book to the moment you arrive, every interaction reflects our commitment to being the best in the business. Crisp attire, courteous communication, and impeccable execution.

02
Punctuality

We believe that being on time isn't optional — it's respect. We plan ahead, monitor conditions, and arrive early so you never have to worry about being late.

What happens in the car stays in the car. Our drivers are trained to be discreet, non-intrusive, and fully respectful of your personal space and conversations.

Every detail of your ride is considered — from the temperature of the cabin to the music playing softly in the background. Your comfort is our standard, not an afterthought.
